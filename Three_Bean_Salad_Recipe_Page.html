Three Bean Salad

  • Time: 20-30 minutes
  • Serving Size: idfk
  • Yields: 10 servings
  • Calories: idk I'm not a nutritionist

  • 1 red bell pepper, minced
  • 2 celery stalks, minced
  • 3 mini cucumbers, minced
  • 3 tbsp red onion, minced
  • 0.25 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1 can canellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 0.25 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 0.25 cup red wine vinegar
  • 0.5 lemon, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp honey
  • salt and pepper to taste

  1. Add juiced lemon and grated garlic to a small bowl and let sid while you chop veggies.
  2. Add chopped veggies and beans to a large bowl.
  3. Add the garlicy lemon juice and the rest of the dressing ingredients to a jar or shaker cup and shake well.
  4. Poor dressing over the salad and toss.
  5. Serve with tortilla chips and enjoy!
